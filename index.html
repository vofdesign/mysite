Velocity is the rate and direction of the change in position of an object.

Design is the planning of something to achieve a specific role, purpose, or effect.

...

Ideas start as an amorphic product. The thought and energy put into the development is the velocity. The design then sets the trajectory for visualizing the idea.

Without either of these two components, ideas cannot take shape. With equal implementation, you are only limited by how far you want to go.
