I Design

Design is my passion, and has been in my nature for as long as I can remember. From Legos and Lincoln Logs, to stone sculptures, block print, and architectural drawing, I have amassed experiences in various aspects of art. I would be hard-pressed to think of something I have not tried in this realm.

This same appetite has carried over into my professional career. I have had the pleasure of working the gamut of design, from print and web design, to packaging lines and racing apparel for a professional cycling team. My choice disciplines were classical art and architecture, both of which apply well to my main emphasis in web design, specifically architecture. A website is after all a structure. You design the structure around ease of navigation, intuitive use, and chosen aesthetic principals. It shares the same core concept, you just use different tools.

The best part about design is that you can never know it all. I am constantly learning and seeking new technical challenges and experiences to further expand my knowledge and skills.
